Lucy's Help

Instructions

Type your code on the box to the left and press the "Run" button. Lucy will start doing what you instructed her to do.

Commands

 moveForward(x)

This will make Lucy move forward an x amount of tiles. For instance: moveForward(3) will make her walk 3 tiles

turnLeft()

This will make Lucy turn 90 degrees to her left. You don't need to give in any numbers. Just call this once and Lucy will turn in place without walking any tiles. 

turnRight()

This will make Lucy turn 90 degrees to her right. Just like turnLeft, Lucy will turn in place without walking any tiles.

It doesn't work!!

If your code doesn't work at first, try hitting the Reset button, changing your code and executing again. Sometimes things don't work as they are supposed to because this game was developed in under 48h and there are LOTS of bugs. But usually refreshing the page does the trick :)
